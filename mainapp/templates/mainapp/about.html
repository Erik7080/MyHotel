Welcome To Our Hotel

Innovative technologies within the hospitality sector enable people to find the most suitable Hotel and/or Accommodation. Many platforms allow clients to filter results by price, location and service quality. However, it is very challenging to find platforms that offer highly specific and tailored services.

The goal of MyHotel is to solve this problem. Here, You will be able to select among several services that are not usually present on other platforms. For instance, You can choose from fixed room prices according to the dimension and build on them Your price, based on the extras You would like. With MyHotel is possible to choose among different amenties of various type, from the mini-bar to a last generation console. This is likely to offer a more tailored experience as price will change accordingly. From the student to the business man everyone can find his perfect solution.

This will increase the efficency of the Hotel under four main aspects:

Availability

Our trained stuff will always be 24/7 available for your necessities

Reliability

Thanks to our secure website your information are safe

Convenience

Only take what is needed, without paying for useless services

Tailored service

You build your experience, choosing only you needed services
